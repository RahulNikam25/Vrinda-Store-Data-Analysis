# 🛍️ Vrinda Store Data Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing the annual sales data of Vrinda Store (2022) to uncover key business insights, customer behavior patterns, and revenue trends.

The goal is to transform raw data into a meaningful, interactive dashboard that supports data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze monthly revenue trends
- Identify top-performing states and sales channels
- Understand customer demographics (age & gender)
- Evaluate order status and delivery performance
- Generate actionable business insights

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Techniques Used
- Microsoft Excel
  - Pivot Tables
  - Pivot Charts
  - Slicers (Filters)
  - Dashboard Design
- Data Cleaning & Transformation

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 KPI Metrics
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Top Performing Category

### 🔹 Visual Insights
- 📈 Monthly Revenue Trend
- 👩‍🦰 Revenue by Gender
- 🗺️ Top 5 States by Revenue
- 📦 Order Status Distribution
- 🛒 Orders by Sales Channel
- 👥 Age Group vs Gender Analysis

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Women contribute approximately **64%** of total sales
- Amazon is the leading sales channel (~35% of total orders)
- Maharashtra generates the highest revenue among all states
- Majority of orders (~92%) are successfully delivered
- Adult age group contributes the most to overall sales
